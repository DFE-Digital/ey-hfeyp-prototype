Before you start

You can expect to see various icons throughout the training course.

In your setting

  • The information icon highlights practical tips to use in your setting.

Reflection point

  • The brain icon signals a chance to reflect on what you’ve learned and how it applies to your practice.

Learning log

  • When you see the pencil icon, you can use the space to make notes on the reflection point. Do not enter any personal or sensitive information like children's names. You can view all your notes in your personal learning log.

Learning check

  • You’ll see the question mark icon next to quiz questions throughout the module. These questions are to check you understand what you have learned.You will be able to see if you get these questions right or wrong. Your scores are not recorded and they do not count towards the certificate of achievement.
