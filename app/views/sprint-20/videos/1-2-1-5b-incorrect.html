Child led play can help children to increase their:

  • imagination and creativity
  • concentration
  • confidence
  • communication skills
  • independence
  • social and emotional development

However, children also need to take part in adult led activities. Including adult led activities in your practice gives more opportunities for you to ‘scaffold’ children’s learning, by giving them just enough help to achieve something they could not do independently.
