Brain development in the early years

Neuroscience research has shown us that early childhood is a time of tremendous brain development.

This course, written by paediatric research scientist Dr Jane Reed and nursery manager Kate Smith, describes how a child’s brain develops from birth to age 5 and gives advice and tips for early years educators.
