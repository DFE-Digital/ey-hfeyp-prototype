True or false? Around the age of 5, a child will begin to choose their friends based upon characteristics they would like for themselves.

That's not quite right.

At age 5 children will want to please others and are able to take part in activities and tasks that involve cooperation and direction. They will be choosing their own friends and will see characteristics in friends that they would like for themselves.
