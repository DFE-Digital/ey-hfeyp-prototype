Supporting physical development in the early years

On this course you’ll learn about the importance of physical activity for young children’s overall development.

This course, written by paediatric research scientist Dr Ben Roberts, and nursery manager Kate Smith, describes how a child develops physically from birth to age 5 and gives advice and tips for early years educators.
