Module 1: Understanding child development and the EYFS

As an early years practitioner, understanding child development and the early years foundation stage (EYFS) will enable you to meet the needs of all the children in your care.

On completion of this module you will be able to:

  • explain the importance of early childhood education and care including strategies you can use to provide a positive approach
  • explore the importance of child development knowledge including an awareness of contributing factors
  • consider effective delivery of the early years foundation stage (EYFS)
  • describe the impact that the coronavirus (COVID-19) pandemic has had on children’s development
