Free child development training

You can now access short online courses to help you support the development of children in your setting. Each course has been written by an early years expert, working with the Department for Education and Ofsted.

Each training module:

  • is free for childminders, nurseries and other early years providers
  • takes less than 2 hours to complete
  • can be paused and restarted as much as you like
  • includes interactive exercises, videos and case studies
  • has a certificate that you can downloadudies
